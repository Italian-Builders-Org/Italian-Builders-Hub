§01

The Manifesto

read aloud

Italian Builders exists to connect people who build. Our goal is to create the home for Italian builders of all ages and experience levels — a place where ideas, projects, knowledge, and opportunities can be shared.

We believe talent is not the problem. Across Italy, developers, designers, founders, creators, researchers, and entrepreneurs are building remarkable products, companies, and technologies every day. Too often, however, these people remain isolated, work alone, or never meet the collaborators, partners, investors, or friends who could help them take the next step.

Our mission is to make those connections easier, more frequent, and more natural.

We want to create a space where people can meet, share what they are building, exchange ideas, collaborate, and bring new projects to life together. We believe the best opportunities are born from relationships, and that great projects are often the result of the right people meeting at the right time.

§02

What we believe

six tenets
T.01

Talent is not the problem.

Talent exists in every region. The gap is connection — not ability. We close that gap.

T.02

Builders should not work alone.

Isolation is the enemy of great work. The collaborator you need is closer than you think.

T.03

Opportunities come from relationships.

Not from cold outreach. From the right people meeting at the right time, more than once.

T.04

The medium is secondary.

Software, hardware, AI, content, open-source — what unites us is not what we build, but that we build.

T.05

Connections should feel natural.

Forced networking rots. We design for serendipity — meetings that turn into projects.

T.06

Italy deserves its own house.

From Bolzano to Palermo, builders exist everywhere. They deserve a home that is theirs.

T.07 — The closing tenet

What unites us is that we choose to build.

Everything else — the craft, the city, the company stage, the age — is detail. The act of choosing to build is the only membership requirement, and it is enough.
